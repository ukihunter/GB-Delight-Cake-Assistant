# 🎂 GB-Delight-Cake-Assistant

GB-Delight-Cake-Assistant is a simple chatbot designed to assist users with everything related to cakes — from recommendations and baking guides to troubleshooting and learning fun facts.

---

## ✨ Features

- 👋 Greeting support
- 🍰 Cake recommendations
- 📖 Step-by-step cake guides
- 🩺 Cake troubleshooting (Cake Doctor)
- 🎓 Learning function for cake knowledge

---

## 🧠 Prompt Guide

### 👋 Greeting

Start a conversation with:

- hi  
- hello  
- thanks  

---

### 🍰 Cake Recommendation

Ask for cake suggestions:

- recommend me a cake  
- recommend me a vanilla cake  
- recommend me a chocolate cake  

---

### 📖 Cake Guide

Get help making cakes:

- i want to make the Chocolate Birthday Delight  
- i want to make the Vanilla Dream  
- i want to make the Red Velvet Romance  

---

### 🩺 Cake Doctor

Fix common cake problems:

- my cake is flat  
- my cake is dry  

---
